Attention Modeling

NeuroVision predicts early visual attention within the first 1–2 seconds of exposure — the only window where eye-tracking simulation is scientifically generalizable.

Based on computational saliency research and cognitive science, we estimate:

  • •Attention heatmap
  • •Distribution of attention
  • •Competing elements
  • •Cognitive overload
  • •Ignored content
  • •and many more metrics...

Most neuromarketing tools stop here. We don't.

Interpretation Simulation

Attention only explains what is seen. It does not explain what is perceived and understood.

Beyond the first seconds, perception becomes interpretation — shaped by language, culture, expectations, and prior knowledge.

NeuroVision uses agentic demographic modeling to simulate how different audiences interpret your design:

  • •Emotional Response
  • •Message clarity
  • •Brand perception

Model up to 10,000 simulated respondents in minutes. Export structured summaries or full CSV datasets.

Data-Driven Generation

  • •Automatically resize for every major platform
